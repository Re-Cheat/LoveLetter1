I am committed to you, to us, and to the future we are building together. My love for you is steadfast and unwavering, and I promise that I will never leave you.

No matter what challenges we may face, I will always be by your side, holding your hand and supporting you through thick and thin. You are not just my partner but also my best friend, my confidant, and my soulmate.

Together, we can conquer anything. I believe in us, in our love, and in the beautiful journey that lies ahead. I promise to always communicate with you, to listen to you, and to work through any difficulties with patience and understanding. Our love is worth fighting for, and I will always choose you, again and again, every single day.

Thank you for being the incredible person that you are and for loving me so deeply. I am truly blessed to have you in my life, and I look forward to creating many more wonderful memories with you.

With all my love and forever yours,

Earl L. Acosta
